My past camping trips & photograghy documenting the expirence.

Garner State Park

This camp site was located at Garner State Park, a west Texas state park.
Garner state's water is crystal clear...you can see straight to the bottom with the only obstruction coming from the water's riples. I highly recomend visiting.

Enchanted Rock

This trip was to Enchanted Rock. Enchanted rock is a great mild hiking spot, perfect for beginers.
Along the hike you'll encounter wildlife and enjoy the beautiful views of the west Texas hill country...
..all that fun can make it easy to forget to hydrate, be sure to bring plenty of water!

West Texas Road Trip

This photo was taken on a west Texas road trip.
The road trip itself was a spontanious backpacking adventure.
We simply pulled over and camped out on the side of the road.
It is incredibly quiet out there and no civilization for miles.
